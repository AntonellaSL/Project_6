Our Services

We offer a range of therapeutic and educational programs centered around planting. Our goal is to support survivors of abuse as they heal from post-traumatic experiences and to inspire young children with the joys of growing their own plants. Through the act of nurturing living things, we help individuals find peace, resilience, and a connection to the healing power of nature.

Botanical Gift Box Set

To help both survivors and young learners get started, we provide a special Seed the Hope Box. Each box is thoughtfully curated to include everything needed to begin planting and growing at home. Each box includes:

  • Seeds for fruits, vegetables, and flowers
  • A bag of soil and pots for planting
  • A cheerful, uplifting card to brighten your day
  • A personal notebook for your thoughts, ideas, reflections, and observations
  • A small booklet with instructions, tips, and additional information on caring for your plants
Our planting boxes are designed to create an easy and therapeutic entry into the world of indoor gardening, offering an engaging way to heal and learn.

Mindful Planting and Journaling

In addition to planting, we encourage participants to use the notebook included in our box to journal their thoughts, ideas, and reflections. Writing alongside planting promotes mindfulness, helping individuals track both their emotional journey and the growth of their plants. Program Features:

  • Guided journaling prompts to encourage self-reflection
  • Tips on combining planting with mindfulness practices
  • Encouraging a deeper connection with nature and one’s inner self

One-on-One Support

We offer personalized guidance to those seeking more support in their planting journey. Whether you’re facing challenges with your indoor garden or want emotional assistance, our team is available to provide confidential, compassionate support. What is Included:

  • Tailored gardening tips and advice
  • Emotional support and resources for survivors
  • Recommendations for local services and mental health counselling

Educational Planting Programs for Kids

We are passionate about introducing children to the wonders of nature through planting. Our educational programs teach kids the basics of gardening, responsibility, and environmental stewardship. Through hands-on activities, we inspire a love for nature and the excitement of watching plants grow. Key Benefits for Kids:

  • Learning about the life cycle of plants and how to grow their own food
  • Engaging activities that build responsibility and patience
  • Encouraging curiosity and a love for the environment

Senior Program

We understand that seniors can also benefit from the healing and calming effects of planting. To support their well-being, Seed the Hope offers a special Senior Pack tailored to older adults in senior houses or assisted living facilities. This pack provides a therapeutic way for seniors to engage with nature, boost their mental health, and enjoy the simplicity of nurturing plants. What’s Included:

  • Easy-to-grow seeds for herbs, small vegetables, or flowers
  • A bag of soil and pots for indoor planting
  • Larger-print instructions with clear, step-by-step guidance
  • A cheerful card with positive messages
  • A personal notebook for recording reflections, garden notes, or memories
  • A small booklet with additional gardening tips, plant care, and advice
